Our Story

Two Friends. One Craving. A Movement.

"Meji" means "two" in Yoruba — born from the vision of two friends who wanted one thing: the taste of home without the hours in the kitchen.

Founder Stephanie Olubajo left a career at Boston Consulting Group to build something that mattered. She saw what millions of busy West Africans in the diaspora felt every day — that craving for authentic jollof, but no time to make it.

Her answer wasn't to compromise. It was to obsess over every grain, every spice, every second — until she had a 90-second Jollof Rice that tasted like it took 90 minutes.

"Cultural food shouldn't be gatekept by time." — Stephanie Olubajo, Founder
