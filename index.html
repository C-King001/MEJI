Our Story

Two Friends. One Craving. A Movement.

"Meji" means "two" in Yoruba — the name born from two friends sitting in a tiny London kitchen, both tired of choosing between authenticity and convenience.

Stephanie Olubajo walked away from a career at Boston Consulting Group to answer a question no one else was solving: why should the diaspora have to choose between authentic West African food and a real life?

She spent months testing, tasting, and obsessing over every grain and spice — until she had something that tasted like her mum's Sunday pot, ready in 90 seconds flat. Now stocked in 400+ stores across the UK.

"Cultural food shouldn't be gatekept by time." — Stephanie Olubajo, Founder & CEO
